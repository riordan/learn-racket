Process

Theme Selection: Email me your proposed theme by the specified deadlines. A title or a sentence suffices. I will synchronize if there is a conflict.

Theme Citations: Email me bibtex entries (including urls) and abstracts for your proposed readings by the specified deadline. Send me five to ten. I will prune to the best of my knowledge and I may ask you to search some more.

Paper Copies: Drop off paper copies of the papers that we agreed on by the specified deadline.

Work with your partner

Check Up: Email me your write-up (PDF) at least one week before your presentation is due. At this point, also make an appointment to meet me several days before your presentation to check in with me.

Repeat for second theme.
