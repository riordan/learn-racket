Program Analyses: A Consumer's Perspective and Experiences

Matthias Felleisen

Over the past six years, my team has adapted static analyses for several purposes, including compiler transformations and static debugging. All of the tools have been used in research and teaching. My talk will first relay our experiences, both positive and negative. Based on this experience report, I will then suggest some constraints on future research in this area.
