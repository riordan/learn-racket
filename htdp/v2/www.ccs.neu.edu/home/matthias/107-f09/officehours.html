The purpose of Drill Club is to get help for those students who help themselves. Students who wish to participate -- and everyone who wishes to obtain a good grade should wish to participate initially -- study the book and the drill exercises as early as possible. For all the questions they have, they stick yellow 'stickies' into the book and onto their solutions of some of the drill exercises. Then they go see Felix on Mondays at 4pm sharp, and he will deal with the questions, plus he will conduct code walks with the solution (attempts).

The price for participation in Drill Club is a printed copy of some of the drill problems of the week's assignment set. If you can't pay the price, you're not admitted.
