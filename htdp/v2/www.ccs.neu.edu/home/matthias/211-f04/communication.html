Communication

You must read the Com211 BLOG on a daily basis. See the BLOG tab on the left.

In addition, you should read the newsgroup on a daily basis. We will introduce newsgroups in the first and second lab. The newsgroup exists for people to communicate with the rest of the class about lecture material, lab material, general points of a homework assignments though no specifics, etc. Note: You need to have a CCS account for using the newsgroup. If you do not have one, print and follow the account form instructions of the CCS Systems Maintenance group. Drop off the filled-out form with the Systems group on the third floor (end of West Wing).
