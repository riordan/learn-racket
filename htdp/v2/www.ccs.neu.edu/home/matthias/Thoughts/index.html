This web site isn’t a blog. It is just a collection of thoughts and arguments that I have had to make many times over the years. So instead of formulating them again and again, I have started to write them up and, when needed, I send people a link to the relevant part.

If you like any of these entries, feel free to link to them.

If you think I should know of your reaction, put it on your web page, link to my page, and send me the link. I will respond, and I may connect my page to yours.

Enjoy.
